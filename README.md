## 🛠 Features (The "Stupid" Stack)

### 1. 🔐 Obstacle-Course Authentication
* **Mood-Locked Login:** Access is denied if you select "I’ll do it tomorrow" (because we respect your boundaries).
* **Teleporting Auth:** The "Log In" button physically evades the cursor if your password isn't "nightmare-compliant."
* **Reverse Progress Loading:** A high-fidelity loader that counts from **100% down to 0%** while "Undoing your productivity."

### 2. 📋 Task Delay Manager (The Kanban Graveyard)
* **Columns:** *Maybe Later*, *Definitely Later*, and *Not In This Lifetime*.
* **Recursive Rescheduling:** A "Postpone" button that uses **Gemini AI** to generate professional-sounding excuses (e.g., "Waiting for the alignment of Jupiter").
* **Sluggish Physics:** Dragging a task card feels physically "heavy" to reflect your lack of motivation.

### 3. 📅 Calendar of Avoidance
* **Magnetic Repulsion:** Dragging a task to "Today" automatically slides it to the following week.
* **Auto-Populated Holidays:** Every Tuesday is "International Napping Day."
* **Weekend Buffer:** Fridays are a "No-Work Zone" that glow with a protective neon aura.

### 4. 📊 Overkill Analytics (The Masterpiece)
* **Recursive Regret Sunburst:** A multi-layered radial chart mapping your distractions.
* **Global Avoidance Globe:** A **Three.js** 3D wireframe globe showing "Live Avoidance Events" worldwide via WebSockets.
* **Career Decay Bell-Curve:** A dynamic graph predicting your future job title (CEO vs. Professional Beach-Bum) based on session inactivity.

### 5. 🤖 ProcrastiBot (AI Coach from Hell)
* **Sassy Logic:** Integrated **Gemini AI** chatbot that roasts your focus levels in real-time.
* **Excuse Generator:** Creates copy-pasteable excuses for teachers/managers (e.g., "My laptop is on a spiritual retreat").
* **Panic Mode:** A global `Esc` key listener that instantly swaps the UI for a fake Microsoft Excel spreadsheet.
